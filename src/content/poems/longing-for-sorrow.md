somewhere
sorrow fills grassy meadows
somewhere
over the picket fences
the horses cry 
somewhere
the colors of the rainbow are
smeared and faded
somewhere
the clouds make
mirrored puddles in the mud
somewhere far away
in another's world
the sky is full of fog and
the air is bleak
for once i can truly say
it's peaceful on this side of the stream
