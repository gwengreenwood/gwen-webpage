goodnight my dear                 

have a beautiful dream          

where you’re happy and free 

be what you want to be          
 
goodnight my dear                 

sleep tight, beneath the stars 

and under the moonlight       

don’t let the bedbugs bite      
 
goodnight my dear                 

i hope to see you soon            

when the night disappears     

and morning light is here       
 
goodnight my dear

just close your eyes and rest

you’ll float above the ground

where peace is all around
 
maybe some night                 

when sleep has come for me   

i’ll be in heaven too               

and find myself with you       
 
dedicated to Robbie
