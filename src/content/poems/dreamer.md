there is a dreamer of the world          

who dreams of things that are unheard

he dreams of peace and love endured

this peaceful dreamer of the world    
 
there is a dreamer of this place          

who hopes to find a way someday    

to cure the deeply saddened face       

this hopeful dreamer of this place     
 
there is a dreamer of this land           

who looked around, and then he said,

“i’ll change the cruel and hate-filled man”   

this naive dreamer of this land
