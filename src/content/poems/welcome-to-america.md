Welcome to America, the country of chaos.
Welcome to America, the nation of no trust.
Welcome to my home, where there is rich and poor.
Welcome to the place of death, we’re killing each other off.

Welcome to America; pollution fills the sky.
Welcome to America, there's bullets flying by.
Welcome to America, there’s robberies every day.
Welcome to my home, my place,
I’m so ashamed to say.

Where’s the hospitality? We’ve closed the open doors.
Why must we shed blood in unnecessary wars?
What happened to the truth and justice?
What happened to the peace?
What happened? Oh! What happened,
to this land of liberty?

So, if you come to my home,
“My country ‘tis of thee,”
don’t be surprised if your freedom costs--
freedom isn’t free.
