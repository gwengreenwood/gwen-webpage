one is mad
and so are you
the midnight sky
is morbid too

knights go riding
up the hill
dragons kill
yet all is still

unicorns grazing
wizards extinct
the moon doesn’t care
what anyone thinks

arrows are shot
through bows of hearts
all that you feel
is torn all apart

mirrors break
mountains fall
the people escape
through the hole in the wall

and I will see
the moon tonight
eclipse the sun
that burns so bright

play your harp
and I shall sing
to all believers 
land and sea
