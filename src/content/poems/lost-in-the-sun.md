her crowded wagon of 
dolls and dandelions is
missing a wheel
dried specks of mud cover 
her already-freckled face
grass goes past her knees
shoes are long lost
her sundress is dirty and
unraveling at the seams
her long, orange hair is
knotted, some matted to 
her damp skin
trying to pull her 
broken wagon in the sun
through the flies
lost in the sun
lost all of her hope
falls asleep in the sun
in her muddy torn dress
