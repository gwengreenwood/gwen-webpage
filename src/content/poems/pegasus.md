flying high, i see your skies

the ocean life is mysterious

mountains rise, strong and beautiful
 
this fragile glass world

covered with snow,

is enchanting

the air is scented with wonder
 
you are perfect, earth

but the human race is coming

to strip away your beauty

kill your creatures

and soak you in hate and pollution
