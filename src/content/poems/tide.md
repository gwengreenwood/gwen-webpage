the day i was born
was the day i started to die
on the outside, i laugh and smile
on the inside, i always cry

the day is almost gone
the night is almost here
i sit alone in my room
collecting all my tears

i have an ocean now
so long as well as wide
i sit along the shore
and i'm swept up by the tide

i sink down to the bottom
watch the world fade from view
i realize that dying is
all that's left to do
