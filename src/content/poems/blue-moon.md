staring into space
and meeting only peace
as my mind travels far
too far; too far to see

the moon is oh, so blue
and the stars are golden-pink
my eyes will bounce from dusk till dawn
too much; too much to think

the dragons always fly
and the faeries always play
the harps; the harps are beautiful
too much; too much to say

tonight the wizard’s chanting
to the moon so blue
my thoughts are oh, so pure
too pure; too pure for you
