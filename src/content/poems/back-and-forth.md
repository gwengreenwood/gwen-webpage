a broken pendant
tossed in an overflowing junk drawer
i feel worthless, in a meaningless life

a carousel
spinning around
i circle repeatedly

when your god is presented to me
these thoughts are abandoned
your god charms me
when i look up he's gone

a swing 
on a playground
i go back and forth

a child
drawn to a candy store
i am enticed, but walk away each time
