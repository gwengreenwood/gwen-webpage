It is so wonderful
A love that seems unreal
If she only knew
Why she feels what she feels

He is everything to her
Her moon, her stars, her world
In her heart, she knows he’s special
In her mind, it’s been unheard

When they are together
Her joy fills the sky
the joy is everywhere
But in her lover’s eyes
