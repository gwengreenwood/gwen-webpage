sand beneath my feet
sinking
now drowning in the quicksand
far from being sad
been waiting for the
best option for far too long now
this seems to be fast
too fast perhaps
not as fast as you
how did you handle it afterwards
did you remember 
did you cry
did you feel the pain
did the blackness come crashing in
or did you float into the light
did you hear the angels sing
or did you hear the demons moan
did you take the glass with you as a memorabilia
or did you leave yourself behind
do you still cry
or do you smile
do you hate
or have you forgiven me
i'm so sorry

dedicated to Robbie
