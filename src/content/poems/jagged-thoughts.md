sand beneath my feet…sinking

now drowning in quicksand

been waiting for the right moment

this seems like it would go by fast

not as fast as you
 
did you feel the pain?

did you cry, did you scream?
 
did the blackness come crashing in?

or did you float into the light?
 
did you hear the angels sing?

or did you hear the demons moan?
 
did you take the glass with you?

or did you leave yourself behind?
 
do you cry or do you smile?

do you hate me?

or will you forgive me one day?
 
i’m so sorry

to Robbie
