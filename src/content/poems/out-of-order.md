the colors are
blending together

thoughts aren’t new
just rearranged
shuffled into something
unrecognizable

may i exchange my eyes?
new windows are needed

everything overlaps
the rules aren’t being followed
everything is crossing over the lines

i can’t see—it’s too loud
please, someone fix me
so i can live in this world
