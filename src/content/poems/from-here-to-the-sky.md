once there was peace from here to the sky
and beauty from the sun to the moon
then the sky turned grey as you flew away
and now I don’t know what to do

now there’s only rain
falling here from in the sky
as my eyes fill up with dew
and the fog will soon fill up the space
from the sun down to the moon

I guess I’ll never learn…

that the grass is never green
and the flowers soon are dead
all brightness soon turns dull
and my eyes are always wet

and all beauty soon turns grey
and the sky is never clear
and the days are always turning
into nights that bring me tears
