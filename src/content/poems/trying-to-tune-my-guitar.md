someone, please play me a song
a song of hope, a song of belonging
i still have yet to find a melody
the songs that i create seem to
always be off-key
i cry beneath a broken bridge
my fingertips are stained from
wiping the tears away
i pick myself up and step out from
underneath the broken boards
i bend my neck to look up
everyone seems so far away
i look back down
my body is cracked and warped
if i could play on the same key as
everyone else, perhaps i’d be
joined in harmony
someone, please play me a song
a song of hope, a song of belonging
