i’m so sorry

for everything i did

i’m sorry i made you fight

i’m sorry, i was a bad kid
 
i’m sorry for your alcohol

and the bruises you gave to mommy

but i wish you didn’t run away

i wish that you still loved me
 
please come home, daddy

i promise i’ll be good

even though i’m still a little girl

i’ve grown up a lot

but i don’t know what to do
 
because i love you

you’re the only dad i knew

i’m sorry i made you hate me

i’m sorry i made you so cruel
 
i know it was my fault

for you and mommy getting divorced

but maybe if you come back

we can change it and make it work
 
mommy is so sad

she cries all the time

and i know it’s all my fault

she says her tears are mine
 
so, please come back, daddy

please make it right

it’s not the same anymore

with you not in my life
